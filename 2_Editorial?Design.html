    "THE CHAIN"
    / ACCESSORY DESIGN
    ?Design is a self-published book, centered around graphic design and visual communication. Collected and edited by design student Cecilia Song from her weekly readings in Core 2 typography course at Parsons School of Design, it covers multiple persperctives to help people understand design in the 21st Century, by browsing through the past, present and future of visual culture and creative industry. Rather than discussing over design skills and techiniques, this book focuses more on the critical, creative and responsible mind set that requires for a deep, thoughtful designer. Innovative design form and visual trypography responses are included. Photo editing and texts by Cecilia Song.
